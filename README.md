The year follows a structured 12-month plan covering stochastic calculus, market microstructure, statistical learning, and a sequence of quant projects.

## What's in this repo

- [`weekly-logs/`](weekly-logs/) — weekly progress notes (what I read, what I understood, what's next)
- [`notes/`](notes/) — distilled summaries of textbook chapters as I finish them
- [`reading-list.md`](reading-list.md) — core books I'm working through
